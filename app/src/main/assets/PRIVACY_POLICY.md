# Privacy Policy

GoFlo is free, with no ads, no corporate sponsorship, no cookies, no cloud storage. I don't sell your data. I can't even access your data and I've done my best to ensure nobody can without your consent.

GoFlo does not collect or access data from your phone. It requires notification and alarm permissions only if you choose to use those functions.

All data is stored locally to you and excluded from Android backups, so if you ever change your phone you have to port your own data across.

I do not collect usage statistics. I have no idea how you use the app or what you use it for.

You're encouraged to join me on Discord and give me feedback, report bugs, or suggest features or improvements.
